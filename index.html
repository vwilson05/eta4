Our Mission

Breaking down language barriers, building up futures

English Through Academics, Athletics and Arts Abroad

Since 2009, eta4 has been dedicated to providing free, high-quality English education to students in Vietnam. Our unique approach combines traditional academics with athletics and arts, creating an immersive and engaging learning environment.

From 2009 to 2019, our volunteer-driven 5-week summer camps taught over 14,000 students in cities like Hue, Da Lat, Bien Hoa, and Duc Linh (Phan Thiet Province) in Vietnam, as well as Taitung in Taiwan. After a pause due to COVID-19, we're excited to restart our programs in Summer 2026 in Hue.

Building Lasting Impact Through Local Empowerment

We know that 5 weeks is just a blip in time. The real, lasting impact happens on the ground every single day — long after our international volunteers return home. That's why we focus heavily on recruiting, training, and empowering local volunteers in each community.

Our "teach to fish" mentality means we invest deeply in training local teachers and volunteers, providing them with the skills, materials, and confidence to continue English education year-round. We don't just teach students — we build sustainable programs that live on through the dedication of passionate local educators who understand their communities best.

Our Programs

5 weeks that change everything

Week 1-2

Foundation Building

Students are assessed and grouped by skill level. We focus on building confidence and establishing core vocabulary through interactive games and activities.

Week 3-4

Skill Development

Intensive learning through our three pillars. Students engage in academic lessons, sports activities, and creative arts projects, all conducted in English.

Week 5

Celebration & Showcase

Students demonstrate their progress through performances, presentations, and competitions. Families and communities celebrate their achievements.
